Q.

What is HiveBuddy, and how does it work?

HiveBuddy is a small box that incorporates an Arduino board with temperature, humidity, weight, and microphone sensors. These sensors provide real-time data on hive conditions, allowing beekeepers to optimize honey yield and monitor the health of their colonies.

Q.

For whom HiveBuddy was created?

The Smart Beehive caters to a diverse audience, including commercial beekeepers, agriculture enthusiasts, and newcomers to beekeeping. Its comprehensive features and affordability make it suitable for beekeepers of all sizes.

Q.

How can I access data from the Smart Beehive?

You can access real-time information for efficient hive management through a user-friendly website interface. The Smart Beehive provides the information you need to make informed decisions and improve the overall well-being of bee colonies.
